⛳ Tournament Format

🎯 The Game

Two-man team best ball match play.

  • Each match spans 18 holes
  • 20 points up for grabs per match (1 per hole + 2 bonus for overall winner)
  • All matches are NET — handicaps determined by GHIN on day of match
  • Play from the blue tees unless all agree otherwise

💵 Buy-In & Prizes

  • $500 per person ($1,000 per team)
  • 1st and 2nd place teams in each flight share the pot
  • Flight winners advance to playoffs
  • Last place penalty: Bottom team in each flight pays extra $250 — goes to semi-final winners

📅 Season

  • Start: April 15, 2026
  • End: Labor Day (September 7, 2026)
  • 4+ months to play every team in your flight
  • Play in any order — just get them done!
  • Incomplete matches = automatic tie (10 points each)

🏆 Flights & Playoffs

  • 4 flights (6-8 teams each)
  • Flights determined by the "Committee" — grouped by who plays together
  • Top 2 teams per flight advance to playoffs
  • Semi-finals after Labor Day (or when all matches complete)

📱 Reporting Results
