⛳ Tournament Format

🎯 The Game

Two man teams in net match play over 18 holes.

  • 1 point for winning the hole
  • 0.5 points for a tied hole
  • 2 bonus points to the winning team
  • All play will be from the blue tees unless everyone in the group agrees to play from a different tee box
  • Handicaps and strokes will be determined the day of the match using the reflected handicaps available on GHIN

📱 Reporting Results

Results of each match should be entered into the Summer Long Hazel website as soon as possible.

📅 Season Rules

  • If a match is never played by the end of the season, each team will receive 10 points each
  • Injury rule: if someone gets hurt or becomes disabled, their partner continues the season on their own with no replacement
  • The last place team in each flight will be assessed $250 per player, paid to the winners of the semi-final match

🏆 Tie Breakers & Playoffs

  • If teams are tied for 1st, 2nd, or last place in their flight, tie breakers are used in this order:
  • 1. Winner of head-to-head match
  • 2. Number of matches won
  • 3. Highest number of points won in any single match
  • 4. Coin flip
  • Top three flight winners by total regular season points advance to the playoffs
  • The two flight winners with the fewest point totals play a play-in game to advance
  • If more than two flight winners are tied on points for playoff spots, tiebreakers are:
  • 1. Total number of matches won
  • 2. Highest point total won in any match
  • 3. Teams drawn from a hat
  • Semi-final pairing: the team with the highest regular season point total plays the winner of the play-in game

💵 Payouts

  • Each flight pays the same:
  • Flight winner: 50% ($3,000)
  • 2nd place: 30% ($1,800)
  • Remaining 20% goes to the overall 2026 champions pot
  • Semi-final winner: $1,500 total ($750 per man), paid from the last place team in each flight
  • Overall winners: 20% of each flight total = $6,000 ($3,000 per man)
